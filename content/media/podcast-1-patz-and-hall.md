They produce some beautiful wines and are now celebrating their 28th harvest. We caught up with Don at the gloriously elegant Bouchon at the Venetian Hotel in Las Vegas after an amazing meal and wines. Donald was as generous in the pod cast as he was with the meal. I hope you enjoy it as much as I did.
