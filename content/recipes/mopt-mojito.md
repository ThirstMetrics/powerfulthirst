I prefer my cocktails more refined than the uniformly messy versions of mojitos served throughout the United States these days. Why on earth would I want a muddled mess of an alcoholic slushy? I am way too vain to run around with bits of mint in my teeth and you should be too. Try this recipe for an embarrassment free mojito experience.

## Ingredients

- 2 oz White Rum (Havana Club if you can find it.)
- 7 large mint leaves
- 1/2 ounce of simple syrup
- 1 ounce lime

## Garnish

- Lime wedge
- Mint Sprig

## Glassware

12 oz double old fashioned glass

## Assembly

Chill glass. Place mint leaves in the bottom of a cocktail shaker and cover with sugar and with the round side of the muddler, smash twice and add simple syrup, lime juice and rum and shake vigorously. Strain into chilled glass filled with crushed ice and garnish.

## Suggestions

If you have access to actual cane juice freshly crushed substitute equal about of cane juice for the simple syrup.
