The Product Suite

Streetwise

Location-pinned photographic intelligence. Field reps record market visits directly on Google Maps, creating street-level data that digitizes menu and inventory information so brands can see market penetration by segment. Works offline, runs entirely in the browser — nothing stored locally but everything accessible locally. A generic platform usable in any industry.

Whiskey River

Texas market intelligence and CRM. Turns public TABC data into actionable prospect lists so reps can sell smarter, not harder. Built-in CRM with beverage menu scanning. Lives at texas.thirstmetrics.com.

Spotlight

Multi-outlet resort inventory management. Gives distributors and suppliers access to depletion data across properties, making reorder cycles and portfolio performance visible in one place.

Cadenza

Beverage menu printing and management software. Handles ordering, tracks margins on each item, and prints polished guest-facing pages — so operators can run a tighter program without the spreadsheet gymnastics.
